Summer Salad

Delicious and Nutritious!

Sunshine on your skin and a cool breeze in your hair. Summer is here and
this salad is the perfect companion to your outdoor adventures. Quick to
assemble and light on your stomach to let you enjoy more of your day and
feel good about it.


Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, cut or torn into bite-size pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) package shredded cabbage
  • 1 1/2 cups golden raisins
  • 1 cup red grapes, halved
  • 1 pink strawberries, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle creamy poppy see salad dressing
  • 2 (2.25 ounce) package pine nuts
  • 2 (2.45 ounce) package sliced almonds

Steps

  1. Mix the lettuce, cabbage, raisins, grapes, strawberries, and onion together in a large bowl.
  2. Add the dressing and toss to coat.
  3. Top with pine nuts and almonds to serve.
