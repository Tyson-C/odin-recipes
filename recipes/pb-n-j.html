Grilled PB & J

What is this?

I picked easy recipes for this assignment because I'm more focused on the
actual web development than the recipe. But what is this? I can't tell if
I like it or not. Toasted peanut butter and jelly. I feel like I need to
make this now to try it.


Ingredients

  • 2 tsp. butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1 tsp. peanut butter
  • 2 tsp. any flavor fruit jelly

Steps

  1. Heat griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Spread peanut butter on unbuttered side of one slice of bread, and jelly on the other.
  4. Place one slice, buttered side down on the griddle.
  5. Top with other slice, so that peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  6. Cook for 4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown, and heated through.
